  A B2B demand-generation and revenue-operations business modeled end to end — from ad spend

  opportunities and the pipeline stages that carry them to close. Campaigns anchor everything,
  paid and non-paid alike: every dollar of ad spend, every web session and every touch on the
  path to conversion ties back to a campaign, so channel performance can be traced cleanly from
  a click to a closed-won deal. Account carries the firmographics and the ABM target-list flag,
  since real B2B deals are won or lost at the account level, not the individual lead level.
